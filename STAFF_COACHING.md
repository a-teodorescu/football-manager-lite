# v2.8 Staff & Coaching Team

Adds a Staff tab with Head Coach, Assistant Manager, Fitness Coach, Scout, Youth Coach and Physio.

Effects included in this prototype:
- staff wages are charged in round finance reports;
- Fitness Coach improves training bonus;
- Scout discounts market/youth scouting;
- Youth Coach, Physio, Assistant and Head Coach are exposed in Staff Impact for future balancing.
